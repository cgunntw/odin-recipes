Homemade Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for homemade lasagna. It will taste good when you make it.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Prep ingredients
  2. Combine ingredients
  3. Put in oven for 25 mins
  4. Take out of oven and garnish
  5. Serve
